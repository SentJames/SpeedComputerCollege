STORY BACKGROUND

The student and faculty portal for Speed Computer College is a project of Sent James A. Espena, developed for the subject Advanced Database.


The project provides a comprehensve digital platform where students and faculty can access personal accounts, to view and upload grades, monitor and track schedules, and as well as to manage their personal information. The portal aims to streamline and enhanced the student and faculty experience by integrating key academic and administrative features into a single and user-friendly interface.
